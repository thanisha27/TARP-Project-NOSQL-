Day 6: Stretch and Breathe

Relax your body and mind with these simple stretching exercises. Hold each stretch for 20-30 seconds, and remember to breathe deeply.

1. Neck Stretch

Gently stretches the neck muscles to relieve tension and improve flexibility.

2. Body Stretch

A full-body stretch that enhances flexibility and reduces muscle stiffness.

3. Child's Pose

A relaxing yoga pose that stretches the back and hips while promoting relaxation.

4. Lunge Twist

A dynamic stretch that targets the hips, glutes, and spine, improving mobility and balance.

5. Hip Flexor Stretch

Stretches the front of the hips to increase flexibility and alleviate tightness in the hip flexors.
