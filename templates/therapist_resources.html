Emergency Resources:

If you or someone you know is in crisis, please reach out for immediate help:

  • National Suicide Prevention Lifeline: 1-800-273-TALK (8255)
  • Crisis Text Line: Text HELLO to 741741
  • Emergency Services: Call 911 for immediate assistance
  • Local Mental Health Services: Check your local listings for nearby resources.
